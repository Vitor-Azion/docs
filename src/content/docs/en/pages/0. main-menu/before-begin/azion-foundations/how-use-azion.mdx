To start using Azion, you must [create a new account](https://manager.azion.com/signup) via **Real-Time Manager (RTM)**.

The [Get started](/en/documentation/products/get-started/) documentation provides information to guide you in your first steps: getting to know the platform, building your first application, completing a few configurations, and checking your metrics.

If you **Choose your journey**, you get to know the products or features of [Azion categories](/en/documentation/products/azion-foundations/azion-products-catalog/):

- [Build](/en/documentation/products/guides/build/overview/)
- [Secure](/en/documentation/products/guides/secure/overview/)
- [Deploy](/en/documentation/products/guides/deploy/overview/)
- [Observe](/en/documentation/products/guides/observe/overview/)

You can go to the [Develop with Aion](/documentation/products/build/develop-with-azion/overview/) section to find the tool that adapts to you better, including [Azion CLI](/en/documentation/products/cli/), [GraphQL API](https://www.azion.com/en/documentation/products/graphql-api-overview/), [Azion Terraform Provider](https://www.azion.com/en/documentation/products/terraform-provider/), [Azion Go SDK](https://www.azion.com/en/documentation/devtools/sdk/go/), amd the [code editor](/en/documentation/products/edge-application/edge-functions/runtime-api/code-editor/).

Azion also offers support for a variety of [frameworks](/en/documentation/products/build/develop-with-azion/frameworks-specific/overview/) to write and deploy your own code to the edge.

:::tip
If you're a company looking for a Business or higher support plan, [contact the Azion Experts](https://www.azion.com/en/contact-sales/). They can assess, plan, and implement solutions according to your needs, helping you to adopt Azion Edge Platform faster.
:::
